The traveler named Henry played a significant role in the bakery's success. After being offered a job by Thomas, Henry learned the art of baking and became an integral part of the bakery. He helped with daily tasks and eventually learned the secrets of Thomas's recipes. Over time, Henry and Thomas developed a strong bond, working side by side and sharing stories. When Thomas decided to retire, he entrusted the bakery to Henry, knowing that he would continue the tradition of excellence and kindness. Their relationship evolved from a chance encounter to a deep friendship and partnership.
